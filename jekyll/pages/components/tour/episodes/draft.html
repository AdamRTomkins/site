Navigate to 'New draft'

Once logged-in, click New draft in the main menu.

Or click this link to open it in a new window.

Pick a pattern to draft

You'll get a list of patterns. Click the one you want to draft.

If you know the name, these's a handy list of all names at the top.

Note the required measurements

The next step is to pick a model.

It's important to note that you can only choose models that have all required measurements.

Choose your model

Next, you'll get a list of all models with the required measurements.

Click the model for which you'd like to draft your pattern.

Pick your options

Next you can configure your draft.

You do so by adjusting the options in the different sections.

About options

The General options are the same for every pattern.

Other options differ from pattern to pattern.

Reset and Help

When you change an option, the Reset button allows you to set it back to its default.

If you are uncertain, the Help button will show help for the option.

Draft your pattern

When you're happy with your choices, click the Draft pattern button at the bottom.

Congratulations, you're done

Your pattern will now be drafted for you.

When it's ready, you'll be taken to the draft's page where you can study or download it.

This is the end of this guide

You can also redraft or fork a draft.
