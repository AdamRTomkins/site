What is this?

This website drafts sewing patterns to your measurements

We also provide detailed instructions on how to sew them into garments.

Last but not least, we share the software that makes this happen.

Whom is it for?

For makers: people who sew or want to learn how to.

For designers who'd like to design using our platform.

For developers interested in our code.

How much does it cost?

Everything is free of charge. We are not a business, but a community project.

In addition, we pledge that all donations go to Médecins Sans Frontières (MSF).

That's right, this website drafts sewing pattern and raises money for charity.

How does it work?

Our software uses your measurements and pattern options to draft a pattern for you on the fly.

It outputs SVG (because SVG rocks), but we can convert it to PDF for you for printing

Or try our paperless theme to save trees, and time.

How do I get started?

Sign up and create a model so you can add measurements to it. Then, simply create a new draft.

Need a hand? Our tour guide has episodes on how to sign up, how to add a model and measurements, and how to generate your first draft.
