Navigate to 'Your models'

Once logged-in, click Your models in the Account section of the main menu.

Or click this link to open it in a new window.

Click the 'Add model' button

We're going to add a model. Click the Add model button to get started.

Enter a name

You need to enter a name for your model. It can be whatever you want.

A name is handy for telling models apart.

Click Continue

Click the Continue button go on.

Pick a shape

If your model has breasts, click the slider to make it slide to the right.

When you're happy with your choice, click the Add model button.

The model page

Your model is created, and you are taken to the model's page.

Now that the model exists, we'll look into configuring it.

Model settings

Click the Settings button to pull up the model settings screen.

Here, you can update the name, shape, units and picture of your model.

Close the settings modal

When you're happy, close the settings modal window.

We're going to add measurements next.

Add measurements

Scroll down the model page to the Measurements section.

Click the plus sign for the measurement you want to add.

Enter measurement value

A modal window will appear where you can enter the value.

Click the Show instructions button for info on how to measure.

Enter measurement value

Enter the value of your model's measurement and click the Save button.

Repeat this for all other measurements you want to add.

This is the end of this guide

You can also add multiple models, or remove them again.
