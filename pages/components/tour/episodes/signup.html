Navigate to the sign up form

Click Sign up in the main menu.

Or click this link to open it in a new window.

Enter your email address

Enter your email address in the field marked Email address.

Enter a password

Choose a password and enter it in the field marked Password.

Click the 'Sign up' button

Now click the sign up button.

After a while a message saying Great, now go check your inbox will appear.

Check your inbox

Check your inbox for an email sent from mg@freesewing.org.

Can't find it? Perhaps it's in your spam folder.

Click the link inside the email

In the email is a link to confirm your account.

Click it and you will be activated and taken to your account page.

You're done

Congratulations, you are now signed up.

This is the end of this guide

If you want, you can re-send the activation email.

There's a link to do so on the bottom of the sign up form.
