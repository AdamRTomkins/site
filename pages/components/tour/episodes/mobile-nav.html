Going home

On every page, you'll find the slanted page header at the top.

Tap the Freesewing header, the text below it, or our logo to return to the home page.

Breadcrumbs

As you navigate the site, breadcrumbs at the top of the page show you where you are.

Tap the breadcrumbs to go to that page. The fisrt breadcrumb always takes you to the home page.

Menu

The menu icon at the bottom right corner opens the menu

Tap anywhere outside the menu to close it again.

Table of contents

On pages with a table of contents, there's second icon left from the menu icon the bottom right corner.

Tapping it will open the table of contents. Tap anywhere outside the table of contents to close it again.

Modal windows

Some content, like these guides or the login form, load in a modal window that overlays the entire screen.

Such modal windows can be closed with the cross icon in the top right corner, revealing once again the page underneath.
