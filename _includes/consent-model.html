Do you give your consent to process your model data?

What is model data?

Body measurements. An optional name and notes.

Why do we need it?

To generate made-to-measure sewing patterns. To help you organize your models.

How long do we keep it?

Until you remove the model, your account, or revoke this consent.

Do we share it?

Yes. We publish anonymized measurement as part of our open data efforts.

Note: You can excercise your right to object to be excluded from this dataset.

Understanding your right to object?
You have the right to object to your data being used for for research and statistics, even when you give your consent to process your model data to generate made-to-measure sewing patterns.

By default, your consent here means that fully anonymized data about your models will be included in our open dataset.

If you do not want that to happen, but still want made-to-measure patterns, then first give your consent, and then object to the use of your data for research and statistics.
