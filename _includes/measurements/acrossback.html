Your acrossBack is the measurement horizontally across your shoulder blades. Think of it as the width of your back without your arms.

To measure your acrossBack, measure horizontally at the widest part of your back, from one side to the other.

The across back can be measured with a straight ruler. Do not include your arms or curve around your body, but measure flat across between where the sleeves of a good fitting shirt would start.
